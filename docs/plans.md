# Plans

Application has two plans: *Free* and *Premium*. The Free plan is the default one, and the Premium plan is a paid subscription that offers additional features and benefits.

Pricing is TBD, but it will be a monthly subscription with a free trial period. Users can upgrade to the Premium plan at any time from the app.
Suggested price for the Premium plan is around 2-5€ per month, but it will be confirmed after further market research and testing.
